## 2. What Data We Collect

When you purchase or activate a commercial licence, we collect:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Email address | Licence delivery, renewal notices, support |
| Licence tier | To determine what you are authorised to use |

| Payment confirmation reference | To verify payment (no card details stored by us) |

payment processor subject to their own privacy policy.

### 2.2 Support Enquiries

If you contact us by email, we collect your email address and any information you
choose to include. This is used solely to respond to your enquiry.

### 2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

Serenity runs locally on your machine. We do **not** collect, transmit, or store:

- Your conversations with Serenity
- Data processed by your Serenity instance
- Contents of your vault, memory, or NNN store
- System information or usage telemetry (unless you explicitly enable a future
  opt-in analytics feature, which will be clearly disclosed)

- **Restriction** — request we limit processing of your data
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw consent** — where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact: **serenitydev32@gmail.com**

We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the
**Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)** at https://ico.org.uk if you are in the UK,
or your national supervisory authority if you are in the EU.
